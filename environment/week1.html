WEEK 1

Our first week began with an old idea I've had that could expand on the classic website model. I saw that the technology for an immersive and interactive website could be made with the existing libraries and systems that are available to everyone today. The link to the brief fits nicely as I believe the theme explores the idea of "liminality" as it examines the passing of one state to another, in this case being the passing of the traditional into the futuristic. After discussing ideas in my group we still felt as this initial idea was the strongest for a couple reasons. Our main attraction to this was bluntly that we think it would look good on a portfolio. The degree of technical ability is quite advanced and would be a good showcase of our skills and talent. I've seen previous examples of these 3D sites however, I believe them to be quite uninspired and lacking artistic aesthetic. Having received the brief on Tuesday with the hand-in on Thursday we did pretty well to get on with an idea. The timezone difference and Chinese firewall made it incredibly hard to negotiate files between our computers however, after much back and forth we managed to transfer. I have much interest in modern technology and felt it slides nicely into our idea, so we began scanning our faces using Record3D and creating a few scenes in C4D. I added these into Sketchfab and began recording some very basic shots and composition for the video. I decided to add some J Dilla into the mix to make it a slam dunk.
